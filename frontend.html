How to Use NearShare

  1. Sign In with your credentials. Use the "Try Demo" button for a quick start.
  2. Once signed in, toggle Enable Uploading to prepare your files.
  3. Drag and drop files into the zone or click to select them from your computer.
  4. Your files are encrypted instantly and saved securely on the server.
  5. Refresh the list to see your files and Download them anytime.
